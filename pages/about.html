What's up! I'm Chase.


I’m a passionate UX/UI designer hailing from the sunny shores of Huntington Beach, CA. With a knack for blending creativity and functionality, I've cultivated my skills not only in design but also in the realms of digital marketing and web development.

I've honed my craft through rigorous training, having completed three intensive bootcamps at UT Austin. These include a comprehensive full-stack web development bootcamp, a dynamic UX/UI design bootcamp, and an insightful digital marketing bootcamp. My commitment is underscored by the certificates of completion I proudly hold for each.

While my days are currently spent as a valet in the picturesque Newport Beach, CA, my sights are set on a vibrant career. I'm on the lookout for a full-time role as either a junior UX/UI designer or an SEO strategist, where I can channel my diverse skill set into creating compelling digital experiences.

What makes me stand out in the world of UX/UI design is my unique background in web development. I believe this dual expertise enhances the designer-to-developer handoff process, ensuring a seamless translation of vision into reality.

Beyond the pixels and code, I find joy in the outdoors. Whether it's casting a line while fishing, carving through fresh powder on a snowboard, or perfecting tricks on a skateboard, I embrace the thrill of adventure.

Join me on this creative journey, where design meets development, and let's craft digital experiences that leave a lasting impact.
